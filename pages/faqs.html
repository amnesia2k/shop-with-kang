Frequently Asked Questions

ShopWithKang provides personal shopping services, including finding and purchasing products on behalf of clients, suggesting new products based on preferences, and handling returns and exchanges.

You can book a personal shopper by signing up on our website and selecting the "Book a Shopper" option. You'll be guided through a quick questionnaire to match you with the perfect shopper for your needs.

Our pricing varies depending on the type and duration of the service you require. We offer both one-time shopping sessions and subscription plans. Please visit our Pricing page for detailed information.
